About us

IAM Shopping Mall, Ghana's no. 1 online retailer was established in May 2014 with the aim and vision to become the one-stop shop for retail in Ghana with implementation of best practices both online and offline. IAM Shopping Mall is the largest online retail store in Ghana. At inception we did an average delivery time of a week, today we do, on average, delivery in 1-5 days. Initially starting with 3 employees, IAM Shopping Mall presently has a staff strength of 80 young and entrepreneurial Ghanaians including our 8 man strong customer service team available 6 days a week. Deliveries to all 10 Regions in Ghana.
